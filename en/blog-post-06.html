Samsung Galaxy S26

05‑03‑2026

The S26 lineup was unveiled in February 2026. It is a premium smartphone family made up of the Galaxy S26, S26+, and S26 Ultra.

📱 What the Samsung Galaxy S26 is

The Galaxy S26 is Samsung’s 2026 generation of premium smartphones. The S-series premium positioning remains, with a strong focus on:

  • Photography (new stabilization technology)
  • Galaxy AI for editing, creation, and image optimization
  • A new Exynos 2600 processor on S26 and S26+

🔍 Available models

Galaxy S26

  • Thickness: 7.2 mm
  • Weight: 167 g
  • Camera: 12 MP + 8 MP (according to Wikipedia specs)
  • Battery: up to 31 hours of video playback (according to Samsung)

Galaxy S26+

  • Dimensions: 158.4 × 75.8 × 7.3 mm
  • Weight: 190 g

Galaxy S26 Ultra

  • Dimensions: 163.6 × 78.1 × 7.9 mm
  • Weight: 214 g

Innovation

Samsung highlights several innovations on the new Galaxy S26:

  • New stabilization technology
  • Better AI integration in photo functions
  • The S26 includes the world’s first mobile privacy display

🎥 Photo & video

Samsung highlights:

  • A redesigned camera module
  • Improved image processing (less noise, more detail, better brightness)
  • AI features that can edit a photo from a simple text prompt

⚙️ Performance

  • Exynos 2600 on S26 and S26+ (Samsung moves away from Qualcomm on these models)
  • Improved NPU / GPU / CPU for AI and gaming

🔋 Battery & charging

  • Up to 31h video playback (S26)
  • Fast charging: 69% in 30 minutes

💶 Positioning and release

  • Announced on February 25, 2026 during Galaxy Unpacked in San Francisco
  • Cleaner design and strong AI integration highlighted
  • Prices considered high by several specialized media outlets

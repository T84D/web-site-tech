My Project

25‑01‑2026

This article is about one of my biggest personal goals.

I would like to go to the United States because tech opportunities are often more advanced there.

I’d love to visit Silicon Valley in California and look for an internship or a job in IT, ideally at Corsair. I really appreciate this brand because I already use one of their keyboards, memory kits, and fans.

I like their design direction and how they present their latest products on social media.

Next year, during my BTS program, I will have a two-month internship period that I would like to do there, even if I know the chances are low.

Right now, I need to plan all the practical questions to reach this goal:

- Understanding company requirements

- Finding the best way to contact companies

- Learning the legal framework for internships

- Entry authorization for the country

- Passport and visa process

- Travel and living costs

- Expected compensation

I am currently signing up for a student messaging platform to connect with people who live there.

I’d like to exchange with them, discover their way of life, share interests, and maybe meet one day.

That is one of the reasons why I offer IT services: to save money and fund this project.

This is a strong personal motivation for me.

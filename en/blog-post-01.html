AMD Software: Adrenalin Edition 26.1.1

Major update released on January 28, 2026.

🎯 New Features

  • Optional AI Bundle: new installer module for advanced AI features

📱 New CPU Support

  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 9 HX 475
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 9 HX 470
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 9 465
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 7 450
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 7 445
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 5 435
  • AMD Ryzen™ AI 5 430

🎮 New Game Support

  • Starsand Island
  • Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora™ - From the Ashes Edition

🔧 Fixed Issues

  • Fixed incorrect shadows in Call of Duty®: Black Ops 7 (RX 5000/6000)
  • Fixed texture corruption in Enshrouded (RX 7000/9000)
  • Fixed Diablo 4 crashes with non-English characters (RX 7000/9000)
  • Fixed light rendering in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 (RX 9000)
  • Fixed Unreal Engine 5.6 editor crash with Lumen HWRT (RX 5000/6000/7000/9000)
  • Fixed intermittent visual corruption on Chromium/Electron (RX 5000/6000)
  • Improved Vulkan window resizing behavior (RX 7000/9000)
  • Fixed audio dropouts during video playback (RX 9000)
  • Fixed sprite rendering in Cassette Beasts (RX 7000/9000)
  • Resolved TAA artifacts in Baldur’s Gate 3 (RX 5000/6000)

⚠️ Known Issues

  • Intermittent crash in Cyberpunk 2077 with Path Tracing enabled
  • Intermittent crash in Battlefield™ 6 on Ryzen AI 9 HX 370
  • Intermittent crash in Roblox Player when task switching (RX 7000)
  • Texture flickering/corruption in Battlefield™ 6 with Record and Stream

📦 GPU Compatibility

Desktop cards:

  • AMD Radeon™ RX 9070 / 9060 Series
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 7900/7800/7700/7650/7600 Series
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 6900/6800/6700/6600/6500/6400 Series
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 5700/5600/5500/5300 Series

Laptop cards:

  • AMD Radeon™ RX 7900M/7800M/7600M Series
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 6800M/6700M/6600M/6500M/6300M Series
  • AMD Radeon™ RX 5700M/5600M/5500M/5300M Series

💻 Supported Operating Systems

  • Windows® 11 version 21H2 and later
  • Windows® 10 64-bit version 21H2 and later

📥 Installation

For RDNA card users, the driver can be downloaded directly from AMD’s official website, or via Auto-detect and Install.

Tip: when upgrading from an older version, use AMD Cleanup Utility for a clean installation.

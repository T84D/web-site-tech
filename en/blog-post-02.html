My New Graphics Card

24‑01‑2026

It was time to replace my graphics card because my RX 580 was starting to show its age.

The card looks amazing, especially with its RGB LED strip and customizable lighting effects. What surprised me the most is the size: I had never seen such a large card, mainly because of the huge heatsinks that keep temperatures very reasonable. It draws over 180W, yet I still can’t push it beyond 60°C.

For me, this was the right time to buy: because of AI demand, GPU prices are rising again. I bought it for €390, and it already increased to around €500 — about €110 saved.

I chose the Sapphire RX 9060 XT Nitro+; it offers excellent performance for the price. With 16 GB of VRAM, it handles 1440p gaming at high settings and feels future-proof for upcoming titles. I didn’t even need upscaling features (FSR 4) or frame generation.
